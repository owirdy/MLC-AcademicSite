## About Me

I am a second-year PhD candidate at the **NUS Vision & Learning Lab** at the National University of Singapore. My research focuses on **computer vision and multimodal learning**, with particular interests in 3D Scene Reconstruction, Neural Rendering, Open-Vocabulary Object Detection, and Efficient Vision Transformers.
